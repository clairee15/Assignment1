センチミリメンタル

Centimilimental <センチミリメンタ> consists of only a Japanese singer-songwriter, lyricist, composer and guitarist, established in 2012. It was initially a band, however the others quit and all that was left, was Atsushi-san. He is the OST composer for the popular summer anime, Given. He is currently under Epic Records Japan, from 2019 till now. Atsushi wanted to express joy and sorrow with his music, and his strong passion in music has allowed him to carve his own path in the music industry.
