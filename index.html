センチミリメンタル

Centimilimental <センチミリメンタ> is a Japanese singer-songwriter, lyricist, composer and guitarist, established in 2012. He goes by the stage name, Centimilimental and is called Atsushi. He is the OST composers for the popular anime, Given. He is currently under Epic Records Japan, from 2019 till now. Some popular songs owned by Centimilimental are キヅアト、冬のはなし.
